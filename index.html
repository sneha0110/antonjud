Happiness is a state of mind where people start to find their inner self and make sure that their hope for them living is still valid. Happiness cam be obtained in many ways but thre best way which I feel is that... gaining it by sharing it. The more we share happiness.. the more we tend to be happy. We cannot find a single person in this world with no problems and who is completely happy. But still people manage to live lives in such a way that they can find happiness in most of the things they do. So such people can share their happiness with those who cannot afford to find their own happy state.

Candles is an NGO, which offers you a chance to share your happiness with others in need. With the pandemic outbreak, many people have lost their lives and many their hopes too. So we are to organise an event called ACCIO, in which you can participate. In this event you can take part in games, quizes, and many more activities and win prizes. The fee collected will be donated to Smiles an another NGO, who work to make changes in lives of people who still have the determination and passion to make their lives beautiful yet again.
